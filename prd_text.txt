1. Product Overview
Product Name: Dekho
Category: Consumer Fintech / Personal Finance / Behavioral Finance
Type: Habit-first personal finance companion
One-line definition: Dekho is a habit-first personal finance companion that helps users understand
their relationship with money, build better financial habits, plan money goals, and gradually
create assets.
2. Core Problem
Most people use digital payments every day, but they still do not clearly understand where their
money goes, how they behave financially, or what they should do next.
ΓÇó Traditional finance apps are often too transactional: they track, budget, and push products, but
they do not explain the user's behavior in a simple way.
ΓÇó Many users want clarity, not pressure. They need a calm system that helps them see their
money patterns before they are pushed into complex decisions.
ΓÇó The real gap is not only financial tools. It is financial awareness, clarity, confidence, and habit
formation.
